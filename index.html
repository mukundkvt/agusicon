About AGUSICON

The Premier Genitourinary Surgery Conference of India

AGUSICON 2026 is the National Conference of the Association of Genitourinary Surgeons of India (AGUSI), hosted by the UP Chapter of ASI in Bhadohi, Uttar Pradesh.

🔬

Expert Scientific Programme

Curated sessions covering cutting-edge topics in genitourinary surgery, delivered by nationally recognised faculty.

🤝

National Collaboration

Bringing together genitourinary surgeons, urologists, and specialists from across India.

🎯

CME Accredited

Earn recognised CME credits through structured academic sessions, workshops, and case discussions.

Conference Highlights

What AGUSICON 2026 Offers

A comprehensive two-day programme designed to advance knowledge and practice in genitourinary surgery.

Expert Faculty Sessions

Keynote lectures and panel discussions by nationally acclaimed genitourinary surgeons and urologists.

Scientific Programme

Evidence-based sessions covering current advances in genitourinary surgery, diagnostics, and patient outcomes.

Hands-on Workshops

Practical skill-building workshops with limited seats, offering direct learning from surgical experts.

Case Discussions

Challenging case presentations and interactive discussions fostering peer learning and clinical excellence.
