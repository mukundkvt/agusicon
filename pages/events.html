AGUSICON 2026 – National Conference of AGUSI

25-26 July 2026
Blessing Garden, Bhadohi, Uttar Pradesh
2-Day Programme

AGUSICON 2026, the National Conference of the Association of Genitourinary Surgeons of India (AGUSI), is hosted by the UP Chapter of ASI at Blessing Garden, Bhadohi. The two-day programme features expert faculty sessions, scientific paper presentations, hands-on workshops, and case discussions covering the full spectrum of genitourinary surgery.
