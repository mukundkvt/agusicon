About the Conference

AGUSICON 2026 – National Conference of AGUSI

AGUSICON 2026 is the flagship national conference of the Association of Genitourinary Surgeons of India (AGUSI), organised under the UP Chapter of ASI. The conference brings together genitourinary surgeons, urologists, and specialists from across India for two days of intensive academic learning.

The scientific programme covers advances in genitourinary surgery including endourology, reconstructive urology, uro-oncology, and related subspecialties. Expert faculty sessions, live surgical demonstrations, and case discussions are planned across both days.

Delegates will earn CME credits recognised by the Medical Council of India. Registration is open – secure your spot early as seats are limited.
