FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for AGUSICON 2026?

You can register by visiting the AGUSICON 2026 event page and filling out the registration form. You can also contact us directly via email or phone.

Will CME credits be provided?

Yes, CME credits recognised by the Medical Council of India will be awarded to all delegates who attend and complete the programme.

Can I present a paper or poster at the event?

Yes, we welcome abstract submissions for free paper and poster presentations. Please contact us with your abstract and we will review it for inclusion in the scientific programme.

How can our company become a sponsor or exhibitor?

We offer multiple sponsorship tiers and exhibition opportunities. Please send your enquiry to agusicon2025@gmail.com and our team will share the prospectus.

Is accommodation available near the venue?

The event venue (Blessing Garden, Bhadohi) offers accommodation options nearby. We have negotiated preferential room rates for delegates. Please mention "AGUSICON 2026" when booking.
