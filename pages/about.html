About AGUSICON & AGUSI

The National Conference of the Association of Genitourinary Surgeons of India (AGUSI), organised by the UP Chapter of ASI.

Who We Are

Association of Genitourinary Surgeons of India (AGUSI)

The Association of Genitourinary Surgeons of India (AGUSI) is a premier national body dedicated to the advancement of genitourinary surgery across India. AGUSICON is the association's flagship annual national conference, bringing together urologists and genitourinary surgeons from across the country.

AGUSICON 2026 is hosted by the UP Chapter of ASI at Blessing Garden, Bhadohi, Uttar Pradesh on 25-26 July 2026. The conference features expert faculty sessions, scientific paper presentations, hands-on workshops, and case discussions covering the full spectrum of genitourinary surgery.

Delegates earn CME credits recognised by the Medical Council of India. We invite genitourinary surgeons, urologists, residents, and PG students to register and be part of this landmark conference.

Our Mission

To advance the practice of genitourinary surgery through structured national conferences, expert-led education, and collaborative learning.

AGUSICON brings the best minds in genitourinary surgery together to share knowledge, innovations, and best practices for better patient outcomes.
